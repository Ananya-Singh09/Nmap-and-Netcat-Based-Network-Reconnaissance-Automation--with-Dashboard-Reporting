Billing FAQ

How am I charged for seat changes?

Seat additions/removals are prorated for the remaining days in the billing cycle and appear on the next invoice.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer pro‑rated refunds where required by law and for accidental duplicate charges. Contact support within 14 days.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly?

Yes. The change takes effect immediately and any difference is credited or charged on a pro‑rated basis.

What payment methods are supported?

Major cards, UPI, and net‑banking. Offline invoicing and bank transfer available on Enterprise.
