Opt-Out & Data Removal

We value your privacy. You are in control of your personal information, and you can request to opt out or have your data removed from our systems at any time.

How It Works

1. Submit Request

Click the removal button or email us directly with your request.

2. Verification

We verify your identity to ensure your data is secure.

3. Removal

Your data is permanently deleted within the required time frame.

Your Rights

Right to Access

Request a copy of the data we store about you.

Right to Delete

You can ask us to delete your personal data permanently.

Right to Opt-Out

Opt out of data collection, newsletters, or marketing anytime.

FAQ

How long does removal take?

Data is typically removed within 30 days of your verified request.

Will I get confirmation?

Yes, you will receive an email confirmation once your data is deleted.

Can I reverse a removal?

No, once data is deleted it cannot be restored. You may create a new account if needed.
